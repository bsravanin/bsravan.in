bsravan.in

This website uses a domain from Google Domains, a single e2-micro instance in Google Cloud, and DNS servers and a valid SSL certificate from Cloudflare. Inside the e2-micro instance, nginx is serving static pages and routing WriteBetter requests to a gunicorn instance. The domain costs about $0.9/month (one-time payment for a ten-year registration), and the hosting typically costs about $1/month. (Source Code)
